Isabella Hodges

Who do I think I am?

Isabella Hodges is a senior graphic design student at San Diego State University. She is involved at many organizations on and off campus but she prides herself her school’s student-run newspaper “The Daily Aztec” as Graphics Editor, managing print and designs published on the website. In the future, Isabella hopes to work for NGOs, media publication or branding for businesses. Take a look at a timeline of her work below!:
